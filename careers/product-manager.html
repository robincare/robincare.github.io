Product Manager

October 1, 2017 by Robin Care

Role Overview

As a part of the Robin team, you will be part of a team of doctors, nurses, social workers, and healthcare professionals that are working together to improve the experience of cancer patients and caregivers. We aim to see people fully, beyond their diagnosis or symptoms. We work with clients holistically, incorporating their values, emotions, resources, support network and challenges into how we best educate and support them through the cancer journey. As a Technical Product Manager, you will gather business requirements and work cross-functionally within the team to translate them into tangible, well-defined functional product specifications for the engineering team.

A Day in the Life

  • Write clear and concise user stories, prioritize development items, and coordinate schedules and project milestones with designers and engineers
  • Define new product features that drive engagement and produce outstanding user outcomes
  • Analyze user metrics and turn them into actionable insights, including the use of A/B testing and other analytic platforms
  • Work intimately with client service, engineering, data science, and design teams to specify and deploy new features in an agile, scrum environment

    • What kind of projects would you work on outside of day to day?

  • Help build out our database of content and resources so we can continue to provide personalized, tailored care at a high quality level.
  • Further Robin Care’s mission driven culture by joining the team in volunteer and community events with those who have been touched by Cancer.
  • Work with our customers and clients to provide feedback on features and services to add as we grow, and build deep relationships.

    • What would success in Year 1 look like? What key things would you accomplish?

  • Master the ability to collaborate with team members to accelerate evolution of our platform
  • Maintain a clear product roadmap, and matching high level execution plan
  • Show the ability and capacity to accept and implement feedback

    • Demonstrated education, skills and experience to be successful in this role.

  • Entrepreneurial, technology background with 3+ years of product management experience, with a focus on mobile and web
  • Curious, collaborative, and highly motivated self-starter with meticulous attention to detail
  • Experience analyzing data and applying key learnings to develop results-oriented specs for engineering and design teams
  • Possess exceptional communication skills (both written and oral), interpersonal, and organization skills
  • Passion for health care and/or familiarity with cancer is a strong plus
  • Respond to challenges with patience, empathy, and tenacity ; never met a challenge you didn’t enjoy trying to solve
  • Have a high tolerance for ambiguity - change is how we grow!
  • Be an empathic critical thinker and resourceful problem solver

    • Values We Admire

  • Whole Person Perspective: We aim to see people holistically - as more than just a role or a diagnosis. We strive to understand all the different facets that make up who we are. That understanding drives empathy and respect.
  • Authenticity: Because we value and appreciate the whole person, we value authenticity. This includes open/transparent, direct and honest communication which in turn builds trust, productive feedback loops and elevates all of us.
  • Striving for the Best: We all strive for the best - both individually and as a community. It’s about what is the best for our clients but also what is the best for each other and the two core values above provide an important lens for assessing what’s best in any given situation. In striving for the best, we expect to be constantly learning, and that mistakes will be made. We view mistakes as a conduit to growth, and appreciate them as an opportunity to solve problems and learn.

    • Who We Are

    Robin Care is a cancer program that helps people take better control of their cancer journey, make better decisions, and get the help they are looking for. Our team of compassionate and exceptional Robins comes from diverse backgrounds in nursing, social work, care coordination. They are backed by evidenced-based best practices, leading technology, and new models of engagement so they can understand each client deeply, build a trusted relationship, assist in decision-making, and guide people through a complex healthcare situation.

    By helping people with this deeply personalized approach we deliver a dramatically better cancer experience that is accompanied by cost savings of 15-30%, which in turn makes cancer care sustainable for all.

    Today we offer our services as an employer-provided benefit to employees and their dependents. In coming years we will grow into other markets to deliver on our mission of providing a better cancer experience to all populations.
