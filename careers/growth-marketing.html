Director of Growth Marketing

March 15, 2018 by Robin Care

Role Overview

We are looking for a Director of Growth Marketing to join our team and accelerate revenue through creative and targeted campaigns across a variety of channels, including digital, print, and field. The Director of Growth Marketing will focus on increasing user signups and building sustained engagement by developing marketing campaigns that drive brand and product objectives. The ideal candidate will have a sense of urgency and be analytical, data-driven, creative, and highly collaborative. This role is both strategic and operational and the Director of Growth Marketing must be able to own the marketing vision as well as roll up their sleeves and execute to ensure results.

Responsibilities

  • Rapidly define, iterate, and scale effective multi-channel marketing campaigns across email, SEO, SEM, social and print.
  • Analyze marketing campaigns (email, direct mail, offline campaigns, etc) to provide insights and recommendations on customer segmentation, product needs, and market positioning.
  • Develop dashboards that articulate insights and visualization into marketing performance relative to KPIs, projections, and historical performance.
  • Oversee the companyâ€™s web properties and marketing automation tools, helping the team with everything from solution selection to continued integration and tuning

    • Qualifications

  • 6-8 years of experience in digital marketing, at least 2 years in a managerial role
  • Digital marketing experience required including expertise in analytics, A/B and multivariate testing, SEM, SEO, Social, and other inbound marketing channels
  • Experience in performance-based agency or in-house marketing teams preferred
  • Experience building and managing cross-functional marketing teams
  • Demonstrated leadership and management abilities
  • Sense of urgency with the ability to deploy and iterate with speed while maintaining a consistent level of quality
  • Strong eye for design and content quality
  • Highly analytical and data-driven
