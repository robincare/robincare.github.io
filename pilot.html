Your personal cancer champion
Navigating cancer is complicated. Having the right help can make all the difference. Robin Care gives you 24/7 access to a team of care advocates that can help answer questions specific to your needs.
HOW DOES ROBIN CARE HELP?
Emotional Support
Counseling, Support Groups, Mindfulness/Meditation
Day-to-Day Support
Meals, Child Care, Transport, Errands/Tasks
Medical
Care Coordination, Symptom Management, Appointment Prep
Finances
Benefits, Insurance, Co-Pay Assistance
Legal
Advance Directives, Power of Attorney, Will
Personal Care
Comfort Kits, Wigs and Prostheses, Integrated Care
