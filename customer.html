YOUR CARE TEAM
Jess, Senior Care Advocate
"Having the unique background of being an oncology nurse and a former primary caregiver to a cancer patient, Robin Care's mission to see people fully and holistically- beyond their diagnosis and symptoms, and beyond what is currently possible with the traditional medical model- immediately spoke to my passion and drive to make the cancer experience better for patients and their loved."
Robin, Care Advocate
"I am excited to be here to support others with their needs from being here to lend an ear whether it is about cancer or just a time when one needs to talk, vent or cry, helping find a ride to an appointment, finding the right support group, helping with the insurance company and overwhelming paperwork to providing information on whatever the need is at the time. Cancer is enough for one to deal with therefore anything to make it less of a burden I am here and happy to provide."
Our team of Robins are highly skilled in all dimensions of the cancer experience. Combined, the team have decades of experience in more than 7 fields. We have expertise from the domains of nursing, psychology, social work, physical therapy, nutrition, legal and insurance areas. For all of the Robin team, not only have they invested their professional lives to helping cancer patients and their families, they also have a deep personal connection to our mission through their own experiences.
