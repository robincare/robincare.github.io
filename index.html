INTRODUCING ROBIN CARE
We bring clarity and confidence to cancer journeys.
Robin Care experts provide support and guidance you won’t find anywhere else.
Your team of personal cancer advocates
Robin Care puts empathetic cancer experts to work for you throughout your cancer journey. Our “Robins” are Care Advocates, highly experienced within the fields of oncology nursing, clinical psychology, social work, nutrition, physical therapy, and legal/insurance matters. Their sole job is to ensure you get care the way you need it, when you need it - conveniently accessible through mobile, web, and phone. Your doctors are focused on your tumor, Robin Care focuses on you.

TRUSTED RESOURCES
Our team of Robins are highly skilled in all dimensions of the cancer experience. Combined, we have decades of experience in nursing, psychology, social work, physical therapy, nutrition, legal, and insurance fields. Our entire Robin team has not only invested their professional lives to helping cancer patients and their families, they also have a deep personal connection to our mission through their own experiences.

We have over 500 resources available to use in assisting our patients. The resources cover a range of topics, anywhere from information on specific cancers diagnoses to helping locate housecleaning assistance during treatment. While we have resources prepared, we are also available to research and locate specific resources that patients may need at any time during their cancer journey. Our Robins stay up to date on treatment protocols and new innovations, so we can assist our patients with everything from understanding their pathology reports to finding a relaxation technique to use during treatment.
